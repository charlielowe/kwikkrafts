About Me




Hi, my name is Kelly. I have been making arts and crafts for over 25 years, with experience making props for professional theatre sets. My background in crafts, painting, and clothes design allow me to finish just about any job to a high level of quality. I am fueled by my passion for creating and designing. I like to both build on my academic foundations in arts and crafts and stay in tune with the latest fashion and designs through the media.
